## Business Problem

- Conversion rate is low (1%)
- Significant drop-offs across the customer journey
- Weak performance in key segments
- Revenue heavily dependent on repeat customers

---

## Objectives

- Analyse customer behaviour and purchasing patterns  
- Identify key revenue drivers  
- Evaluate conversion funnel performance  
- Recommend data-driven growth strategies  

---

---

## Key Insights

- Repeat customers contribute ~93% of total revenue  
- Midweek (Thursday–Wednesday) drives peak performance  
- New customers demonstrate higher AOV  
- International segment shows high value but low volume  
- Weekend performance (Saturday) is underutilised  

---

## Recommendations

- Optimise checkout process to reduce friction  
- Improve product engagement before add-to-cart  
- Retarget abandoned carts through marketing strategies  
- Leverage midweek high traffic for promotions  
- Expand high-value international customer segment  

---

## Business Impact

- Estimated revenue uplift: **£10.78K** from conversion optimisation  
- Additional growth potential through retention and targeting strategies
